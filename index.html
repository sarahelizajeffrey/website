About me

My name is Sarah Jeffrey, I am from the Squamish and Gitxsan Nations. I come from a long line of artists and carvers. I have always been surrounded by artists. I have been an artist for as long as I can remember. Art makes me happy and brings me peace. Jim Charlie is my father and first mentor. I apprenticed under several of my family members over the years.

My Art History

I have been practicing traditional west coast form-line for over ten years. I started learning to carve cedar seven years ago. I've been painting form-line with acrylic paint for over ten years. I have been working with pencil and pen sketching for ten years. I started working with charcoal and pastel seven years ago. I have been practicing watercolor for six years. More recently, I've been teaching myself to bead and create jewelry for two years.
